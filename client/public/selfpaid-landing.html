How it works

Simple.
Powerful. Fair.

Whether you're here to earn or get things done — SelfPaid makes it effortless in just a few steps.

01
📝

Create your account

Sign up as a Task Creator or Task Worker. Set up your profile, add your skills, and you're ready in minutes.

02
📋

Post or browse tasks

Creators post tasks with a reward and deadline. Workers browse by category and apply to tasks that match their skills.

03
🔒

The reward is locked in escrow the moment a task is posted. Workers know they'll get paid. Creators know their money is protected.

Worker submits their work. Creator reviews and approves. Funds release instantly to the worker's wallet.
